this is a quick little tier list of all the hannibal movies, and yes i will be ranking the tv show soon just not now

1: the silence of the lambs

the silence of the lambs truely is a cinematic masterpiece, every scene is impactful and has true meaning, a young FBI trainee called claric starlingb who is set a task of interviewing a criminally insane doctor, aka serial killer, known as hannibal lector, hannibal uses clarice to escape the asylum by asking for a change of prison in exchange for information on a new serial killer, while be moved, he murders two gaurds and escapes, setting out to kill his old aslyums warden and kill even more! its a really good movie in the small series and its the movie that really gave thomas harris the publicity he deserves, these are the reasons this is going in first place for me (also the book was amazing)

2: hannibal (the movie)

hannibal was a certainly interesting take on the book, it missed out on a lot of features from the novel (such as the classic ending of clarice traveling the world with hannibal) which was quite annoying but i think they did a pretty good job on making most of the books features and scenes into a movie, still not the best movie out there.
